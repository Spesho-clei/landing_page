About Us

Our project, Maze Explorer, was inspired by childhood memories of playing classic maze games and the desire to create an immersive and challenging experience for modern audiences.

As a team of passionate developers, we embarked on this journey during our time at Holberton School, where we honed our skills and collaborated to bring this project to life.

This project serves as a culmination of our learning journey at Holberton School and showcases our dedication to creating engaging and interactive experiences.
